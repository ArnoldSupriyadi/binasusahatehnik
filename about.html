Our History

PT Bina Usaha Teknik was founded in 2008 under the name Pacific Pneumatic which is engaged in trading components for industrial machines that use pneumatic or hydraulic systems. In order to expand line of business, in 2013 Pacific Pneumatic open a branch under the name PT Bina Usaha Teknik in providing better after-sales services in the availability of automation products

With our experience since 2008 in supplying pneumatic and hydraulic products. We keep continue to grow in meeting the needs of some of our loyal customers, PT Bina Usaha Teknik is here to provide even better services and availability components for industrial machines in both pneumatic and hydraulic products.
